DONATE PRODUCTS IN NEED TO CHARITIES INSTEAD OF MONEY




The idea of Donation Desk is to create a software that allows an individual to donate to any organization according to their request or need. Here the organizations are also given the option of placing requests according to the products they are in need of. A user can donate according to the requests of the organization or in-case there aren't any requests then the donation can be made to the organization of their choice. When a user accepts a request the required details of the user are taken to make the delivery process smooth. The main roles in this system are User and the Organization. We conducted a survey on existing online donation sites and learnt that these sites take donation in the form of cash mostly and not products. None of the existing sites have the functionality of letting an individual put up a post about the things he would like to donate. Our system gives individuals the option to put up a post about the things they have and they want to donate and details like quantity, quality, etc. When an organization sees such posts and if it requires the product being donated he can reply to that post and further one to one communication can take place.
